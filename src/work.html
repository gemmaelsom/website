Hey there!
I'm Gemma, and I grew up between the Sunshine Coast and Brisbane. I’ve spent over two decades in film and theatre, and I've recently moved into the exciting world of Tech. I couldn't be happier with my decision. After winning the Theatre Award at the Darwin Fringe Festival and writing and producing three productions of my own, I decided to seek out new mediums I could create with. Even so, I largely stayed within the boundaries of a traditional art career, publishing my writing, producing shows, and participating in the performing arts community. More recently, I decided to push beyond the confines of a conventional artist’s career. Since beginning coding, I've found myself willingly spending 12 hours a day working on a project without noticing any time pass.
Outside of coding, I like to read, spend time with my family and pre-Covid I really loved to travel. My favourite things in the world are watching animal rescue videos, my morning coffee and when my partner cooks for me. I am fascinated by the modern society and I have conducted some social experiences through artist residencies to explore this further. I am in my final year of a degree in Sociology, and I'm eager to enter the tech industry and combine my passions to create meaningful work.
Please feel free to peruse both of my CVs, and please reach out to me via the form on my Contact page or on Social Media to discuss any ideas or opportunities you may have.
